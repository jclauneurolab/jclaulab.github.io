Arun Thurairajah

Arun Thurairajah is a Master's student in the Neuroscience Graduate program co-supervised by Dr. Jonathan Lau and Dr. Ali Khan. He completed his undergraduate degree in Life Sciences at McMaster University. His project aims to automate the localization of stereoelectroencephalography (SEEG) contacts using deep learning and convolutional neural networks.
