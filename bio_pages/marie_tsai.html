Marie Tsai

Marie describes herself as an educator who is passionate about human and planetary health. She graduated with a BSc in Kinesiology from the University of Waterloo and is now completing her MSc in Clinical Anatomy at Western University. Her research focuses on data acquisition for construction of a histological atlas of the subcortex. When she isn't lost in the Fields of Forel, Marie enjoys swimming, cycling, gardening, and eating worrisomely large quantities of cheese.
